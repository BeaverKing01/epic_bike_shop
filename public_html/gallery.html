Carbon Fiber Repairs

Lance learnt how to work with carbon fibre from Alex Selkirk who did all the repairs and paint work for Pinarello. Lance has taken council from aeronautical carbon specialists on repair techniques as well as carbon cloth layups. Lance chooses the right layup and carbon cloth to achieve a repair that is not only flawless and beautiful but also stronger on the spot than before. Each carbon repair is different, and requires a different technique of repair and attention to detail that only an artisan can give. Lance's graphic design background, patience and attention to detail are his strengths. If you have a question on carbon, or are in need of a repair, contact us for a solution.
